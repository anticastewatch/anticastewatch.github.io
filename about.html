Anti-Caste Watch

Anti-Caste Watch is a public platform created to track, document, and expose individuals who engage in casteist speech or behavior, especially in public and professional spaces.

Our Mission

We aim to build social and economic consequences for caste-based hate and discrimination — without waiting for slow-moving institutions or government bodies to act. We must build a better society for future generations.

Why This Exists

Casteism is not just a private belief. It directly affects hiring, safety, and dignity. When casteist threats are made publicly — especially on media or online — there must be public accountability.

Employers, professional networks, and communities must take responsibility by refusing to empower caste bigots. It is a criminal offense and should be treated as such.

What We Can Do

  • Document verifiable casteist statements, threats, or actions
  • Record the name, employer, caste, location, and proof for each person
  • Pressure institutions (companies, parties, media, etc.) to take action
  • Track whether the person has been warned, fired, defended, or ignored
  • Create long-term social stigma around casteist behavior

Removal Policy

We believe people can change — but only with time, accountability, and public responsibility.

  • Ages 16–20: Entries are removed after 2 years if there are no repeat offenses
  • Ages 21+: Entries are removed after 10 years of a clean record and a public apology

Early removal requests must include proof of transformation, such as:

  • A sincere public apology
  • Ongoing anti-caste work
  • Surrender or cooperation with legal action

Why Without the Government?

Government action is often slow, and in many cases, politically compromised or run by those with casteist bias. We don't need to wait. Industry bodies, communities, and employers can take action now:

  • Blacklist casteists from white-collar employment
  • Deny public-facing roles to those with a history of caste hate
  • Build pressure through transparency and undeniable proof

This is all about protecting dignity, safety, and justice.
For a better, fairer future for all of us.
